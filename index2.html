OUR INSTRUCTORS

Kaynan Duarte

2X IBJJF World Champion

With numerous world titles under his belt, including 2 gold medals at the IBJJF World Championships, Kaynan is renowned for his exceptional technique, explosive athleticism, and relentless top pressure. His innovative and adaptable style, combined with his relentless drive to succeed, has solidified him as one of the most exciting and dominant forces in the sport of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu today.

Angela Hill

UFC Women's Strawweight Contender

Angela Hill is a seasoned UFC fighter with nearly a decade-long UFC career. Hill's remarkable striking abilities and dynamic footwork are derived from her Muay Thai background. She is known for her relentless pace, high volume of strikes, and unwavering determination, which has garnered a reputation as an engaging and tenacious fighter, consistently delivering thrilling performances that captivate fans across the globe.

Marcos Barreto

IBJJF World Champion

With over four decades of experience in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Marcos Barreto is a revered figure in the sport. As an IBJJF champion, he excels both on the mats and as a dedicated instructor, sharing his vast knowledge and passion. Barreto's teachings embody discipline, technique, and perseverance, making him a respected mentor and ambassador of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
